From Joburg

ETA: 4 HOURS 15 MINS

Take Exit 132 from the N3 Southbound. Turn left past the petrol station and the Linga Lapa (with Biltong written on the roof). The road becomes a gravel road and proceeds straight along it for just over 4kms You will pass Thatchings guesthouse after 2kms. After another 2kms you will see Providence Country Estate on the right-hand side.

From Durban

ETA: 1 HOUR 15 MINS

Take Exit 132 from the N3 northbound. Turn left back over the freeway and pass the petrol station and the Linga Lapa (with Biltong written on the roof). The road becomes a gravel road and proceeds straight along it for just over 4kms. You will pass Thatchings guesthouse after 2kms. After another 2kms you will see Providence Country Estate on the right-hand side.
